Step 1 of 3
Photo Upload Zone
Open any artist or event detail and scroll to the Photos section. Tap the upload zone to pick a photo from your camera roll.
Step 2 of 3
Direct Upload to WordPress
Photos upload straight to your site's WordPress Media Library via the REST API — no FTP, no WP Admin, no size limits from the app side.
File size limits are set by your server's PHP config (upload_max_filesize). Default is usually 64 MB.
Step 3 of 3
Photos Are Saved Inline
Once uploaded, the photo appears as a thumbnail in the record. Multiple photos stack in a grid. Tap to preview or remove.
