Step 1 of 4
Start with the Basics
Tap + Add Artist from the Artists list. Fill in the name, origin city, and booking status. You can add a photo now or later — tap the circle at the top to upload. The only required field is Artist Name.
Artist Name becomes the URL slug on the WordPress site. Use the final announced name — you can change it later, but it will update the URL too.
