Draft vs Published
Every item in the app — artist, lineup slot, sponsor, or event — has a status: Draft or Published. Draft items are saved in WordPress but won't appear on your website. This lets you build out your lineup before going public.
Use drafts to build your full lineup internally, then flip artists to Published one phase at a time as announcements go live.
